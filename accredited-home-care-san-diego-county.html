Why Aveanna Healthcare is your BEST CHOICE for In-Home Care:

Our Hourly Rate

We charge a single rate regardless of your care needs, making it easy to budget and forecast the cost of care (excluding overtime).

We bill your Long-term Care Insurance

For your peace of mind, we will verify benefits and directly bill your LTC insurance carrier so your up-front/out-of-pocket expenses are limited.

Care Management

We offer a No Cost Initial Assessment, establishing a Plan of Care focused on your loved one’s needs, with additional assessments and wellness checks as scheduled.

Flexible Service Options to meet your needs

A 4-hour minimum shift is all we require:

  • Hourly Service: Caregivers work on an hourly basis, starting with a 4-hour minimum daily shift; days and times scheduled by you.
  • Around-the-Clock Care: Care is provided 24/7 by rotating daytime and overnight caregivers in 12-hour shifts—these caregivers remain awake and ready to respond at any time.

We offer the following services and more:

  • Personal Hygiene
  • Meal Preparation / Dietary
  • Ambulation / Transfers
  • Exercises / Assist
  • Transportation
  • Medication Reminders
  • Companionship
  • Memory Care

We offer you choices, security, and confidentiality in your own home.
