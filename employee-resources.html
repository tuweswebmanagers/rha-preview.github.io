Have questions about your W-2?

Find out more information below

How soon can I get my W-2?

  • Paper W-2s will be mailed on or before January 20, 2025.
  • Electronic W-2s will be posted to the Workday Portal by January 15, 2025.
  • Portal link: Workday (link opens in a new window)

How can I get a copy of a prior year’s W-2?

  • All prior year Aveanna W-2s are available in Workday.
  • If you are needing a prior year W-2 for an acquired business, please email payroll@aveanna.com

What do I do if I do not receive my W-2?

  • If you have not received your W-2 by the end of January and are unable to access it via Workday, please contact payroll@aveanna.com

Can I go online and print my W-2?

Yes, any time after January 15, 2025.

To access, follow the steps below:

  1. Log in through the portal (not the app) at www.myworkday.com/psahealthcare/
  2. Click the Pay Icon
  3. Click on My Tax Documents

What if I can’t log into Workday?

  • For active employees, please visit www.myworkday.com/psahealthcare/d/home.htmld then select “Chat with Amelia.”
  • For former employees, please visit www.ahchelp.com or, call us at 1-833-242-4357 option #1 then option #3, Monday-Friday 8am-5:30 pm ET.
